Homemade Chicken Pho (Pho Ga)

Delicious homemade pho that is perfect for those cold chill winter days.

Ingredient list

  • 1 package rice noodles
  • 1 lb chicken legs and thighs
  • green onions
  • fish sauce
  • salt and black peper to taste

    • Directions

    1. Boil chicken in appropriate amount of liquid
    2. Chop green onions
    3. Begin boiling a seperate pot of water
    4. Cook rice noodles in pot for 1m inute
    5. Drain noodles and pour desired amount of broth into bowl
    6. Eat warm soupy noodz
